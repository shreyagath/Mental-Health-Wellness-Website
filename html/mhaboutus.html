OUR STORY

As fellow graduate students at ICCS in 2021, Shreya and Dephney noticed a shift in the way people perceived mental health.
They happened to check out the people around them and websites supporting mental health stability.
They noticed that there are people who work for mental health awareness provide therapists for people going through distress and for people who suffer from mental health issues.
So they thought to build a website where people can enhance their mental health by keeping themselves busy in learning activities they love to do.
Thus 'BLOOM' was born.
We are a group of members who have come a long way working towards helping people out in distress.
Our mission is to make people do what they love to.
We believe mental fitness is about enjoying life by doing what we love and heres a platform which we think will help everyone out to do what we love!
